About Us

LeTflix is ​​a cinema which was first established in October 1997. Currently, LeTflix is ​​a well-known cinema and has many branches around the world. LeTflix continues to make various efforts to develop its business with existing technological advances. Now, we provide streaming service. Where the members can watch famous dramas anytime, anywhere, and on various types of screens and they can watch without distractions like commercials.

Our CEO

Jonathan Kerling is the founder and CEO of LeTflix. Kerling started LeTflix in 1997 in a mall and only has 4 studios. At that time, LeTflix had stiff competition with competitors who at that time already had a well-known name. Kerling remains persistent and focused on developing LeTflix so that it can grow until LeTflix is ​​what it is today.

Jonathan Kerling


LeTflix Cultures


Entertainment is a fundamental human need, it changes how we feel and gives us sameness.


Our core principles, as well as the specific actions and talents that we value the most, are listed below.


The more these principles sound like you and represent people you'd like to work with, the more probable it is that you'll succeed at LeTflix. We wish to amuse the entire planet.


What is special about Netflix, though, is how much we: empower people to make their own decisions; share information openly, widely, and purposefully convey knowledge; are quite open with one another; keep only our most effective employees are retained; try to stay away from rules..


Our guiding principle is that people come first. Who gets rewarded or fired reveals a company's true ideals. There will be more laughing, empathy, and joy if we succeed.

What is it like to work at LeTflix?

Take on Every Obstacle


The totality of LeTflix can be regarded as multiple separate ships united into one, similar to Noah's Ark. It's not uncommon for ships to meet across impediments like thunderstorms and glaciers in the ocean of opportunities; just ask the Titanic. We think that though inspiration might mean different things to different people, the crew's hard work will always keep the ship moving.

Three pointers, all pointing in the same direction


Our rhetorical compass points in three directions: consumer focus, growth attitude, and the idea of "make it happen and make it better." We set our sights on the goal and keep our gaze fixed on it.

Where we are going


Without a map, having the bravery to sail over unfamiliar waters is useless. We use technology to navigate our way around till we accomplish our goal, using our objective as a guide.
